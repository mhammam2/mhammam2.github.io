Vision Statement

DineIn aims to transform the dining experience by creating a seamless connection between restaurants and customers through a mobile application that will allow restaurant customers to view local restaurants, make reservations, and place orders in advance to increase the convenience of a typical dining experience. Our vision is to revolutionize the restaurant industry by streamlining operations, reducing wait times, and enhancing customer satisfaction.

Development Postmortem

What Went Right

  • Successfully implemented major features (accounts, reservations, menu editing, preorders)
  • Effective implementation of layered architecture (UI, business logic, persistence)
  • Successfully integrated HSQLDB for data persistence
  • Gained valuable experience in Android development, database handling, and UI design

What Went Wrong

  • Significant imbalance in team contribution, especially in Iterations 2 and 3
  • Final merge for Iteration 2 was not handled properly, which resulted in broken features
  • Some features pushed prematurely without integration, causing application crashes
  • Missed improvement goal from Iteration 2 retrospective (merge conflict handling)

Time-Consuming Aspects

  • UI design and integration with database
  • Rebuilding and debugging broken functionality due to poor version control

Areas for Improvement

  • Better team coordination and communication
  • Defined roles, task tracking, and proper version control practices
  • Regular check-ins and reviews to ensure accountability and balance

Team Development Takeaways

  • Collaboration requires structure, ownership, and active participation
  • Technical skills alone are not enough, process and team management are critical
